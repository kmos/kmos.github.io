Hi,

I am Giovanni, a lazy pizza fan, which in the spare time is a developer.

Previously, I worked in R&D Department of BMC Software as a Software Engineer engaged mainly on developing capacity and cloud software solutions.

Afterwards, as a Consultant for Agusta Westland, aka Leonardo Helicopters, I was part of the team focused on developing a common platform for cockpit systems.
