Hi,

I am Giovanni, a Neapolitan guy. If we follow stereotypes, I like: pizza, football (maradona) , sea, sail and laziness. Maybe it's true, but in the same time I like to code.

I am actually working in R&D Department of BMC Software as a Software Engineer engaged mainly on developing capacity and cloud software solutions.

Before land there, I worked in many places (like bar), but mainly I spent time with safety critical systems. In particular, I did a research work about cyber threats for unmanned aerial vehicle, at Center of Research of Italian Aerospace. Afterwards, as a Consultant for AgustaWestland, aka Leonardo Helicopters, I was part of the team focused on developing a common platform for cockpit systems.
